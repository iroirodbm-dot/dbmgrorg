Our Academic Approach

Education at the School for the Deaf is grounded in language access, visual learning, and individualised academic development, enabling students to learn with confidence and dignity.

Curriculum Structure

Early Learning

Foundational language development, motor skills, and visual learning.

Primary Education

Literacy, numeracy, environmental studies, and structured academics.

Life & Vocational Skills

Practical skills preparing students for independence and work life.

Assessment & Progress

Student progress is evaluated through continuous, activity-based, and visually supported assessments, respecting each learner’s pace.
