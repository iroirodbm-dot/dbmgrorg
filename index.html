Empowering the Deaf with Education, Skills, and Dignity

Deaf Biblical Ministry (DBM) is a pioneering non-profit organization based in Dimapur, Nagaland, committed to transforming the lives of Deaf children and adults through quality education, vocational training, and holistic community support.

Who We Are

Registered under the Society Registration Act, 1860, Deaf Biblical Ministry is the first and only organization in Nagaland dedicated exclusively to running a residential School for the Deaf. Since 1987, DBM has served as a center of excellence, advocacy, and long-term empowerment for the Deaf community.

What We Do

Education for the Deaf

Free residential schooling from Nursery to Class X, online senior secondary education, teacher training, and awareness programs promoting inclusive education.

Vocational Training & Placement

Skills development in crafts, computing, agriculture, and diverse trades, enabling Deaf individuals to achieve self-reliance and sustainable livelihoods.

Interpretation & Community Services

Professional sign language interpretation, health initiatives, counseling, and cultural participation that strengthen inclusion across society.
