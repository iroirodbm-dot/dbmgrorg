Who We Are

Registered under the Society Registration Act, 1860, Deaf Biblical Ministry is the first and only organization in Nagaland dedicated exclusively to serving as a center of excellence, advocacy, and long-term empowerment for the Deaf community.

What We Offer

Quality Education

From pre-primary to grade 10, with focus on sign language, academics, and life skills — all provided free.

Vocational Training

Skills development in crafts, computing, agriculture, and diverse trades, enabling Deaf individuals to achieve self-reliance and sustainable livelihoods.

Interpretation & Community Services

Professional sign language interpretation, health initiatives, counseling, and cultural participation that strengthen inclusion across society.
